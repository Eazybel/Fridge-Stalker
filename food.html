Spaghetti Carbonara

Category: Italian Pasta

🧂 Ingredients

Ingredient Amount
Spaghetti 200g
Eggs 2 large
Parmesan Cheese 1/2 cup grated
Pancetta 100g
Black Pepper To taste

📝 Instructions

  1. Boil spaghetti until al dente. Reserve a bit of pasta water.
  2. Fry pancetta until crispy. Set aside.
  3. Whisk eggs with Parmesan and pepper in a bowl.
  4. Mix hot pasta with pancetta, then quickly add the egg mixture off the heat, stirring fast.
  5. If needed, add pasta water to create a creamy sauce. Serve hot!
